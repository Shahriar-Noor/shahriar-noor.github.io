LG Ultrawide Video

Software: Adobe Premiere Pro
YouTube Link: www.youtube.com/watch?v=SNcY5Ytj75o&t=1s

Background: Back in 2017 LG Ultrawide Festival Global hosted a video competition for competitors to produce a video where we had to showcase the benefits of using an Ultrawide monitor compared to a traditional monitor with respects to gaming and productivity.

I directed this video for this competition from script writing to shooting and editing the video where I showcased the benefits of using an Ultrawide monitor over a traditional monitor utilizing the cloning effect. I finished in top 50 of this competition.
